The Conundrum of Exercise for Weight Management in the GLP-1 Receptor Agonist Era.

Authors: Lieberman DE, Aslan DH, Heymsfield SB Journal: JAMA (2026) PMID: 42081217 DOI: 10.1001/jama.2026.5537 Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42081217/ Scraped: 2026-05-05

Abstract

This Perspective describes the challenges of prescribing exercise for weight loss, with and without the use of glucagon-like peptide 1 (GLP-1) receptor agonists, and discusses strategies clinicians can use to improve adherence to exercise recommendations.
