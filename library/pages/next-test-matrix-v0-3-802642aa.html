Decision Rule

Advance only if:

  1. Y1-biased mechanism is confirmed.
  2. CNS delivery is verified.
  3. Sleep/circadian stability is preserved or improved.
  4. Mania-like activation is reduced without sedation-only artifact.
  5. DA/glutamate and seizure hard-kill screens are clean.
